About

I lead teams that build magical web software. My sharpest weapons are JavaScript, SQL, and the Apple Pencil.

I am currently head of product @ Rainforest, which helps software platforms integrate payments.

Projects

Payments, especially embeddable UIs
Since 2017, my primary professional work has been in building embedded UIs for payments, at Stripe and now Rainforest. At Stripe, I was a contributor to Elements, Checkout, the PaymentIntent API, new payment methods, PSD2/SCA/3DS2, and the emoji collection. I also contributed to the W3C's Web Payments Working Group and the EMVCo 3DS specification committee, eventually serving as a translator between them to convince them to actually work together.
Public good and open source projects
Education: Pyret is an in-browser programming language and environment for use in the Bootstrap curriciulum, which is built by some wonderful folks who are experts at both pedagogy and programming language design. In 2020 and 21, I spent a lot of time updating their IDE to be shinier, faster, and easier to use.
Federated Wiki is a new, personal version of the wiki being developed by Ward Cunningham. In 2011, I collaborated with him to design and build the core UI paradigm that still powers it today.
