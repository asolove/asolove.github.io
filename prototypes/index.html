Three design directions

Same post (“Preact in pictures”) rendered three ways. Each direction handles the hard cases (long-form prose, figures with captions, code blocks, sidenotes/footnotes) differently.

  • A. Quarto Academic offprint. Three-column grid with body + marginalia. Warm paper, ink, one ink-red accent.
  • B. Broadsheet Italian editorial. Dramatic display type, two real columns of body, full-bleed diagrams with rules and numbered captions.
  • C. Bell (rev. 2) 70s Bell Labs technical journal × Oxide chrome. Sentence-case serif headings, hanging § markers, mono breadcrumb + nav, ink-red spot color, real sidebar of contents / companion / references.
Prototypes are credible sketches, not finished designs. Best viewed at ≥1100px width. Serve with python3 -m http.server from the repo root so the image at /img/preact-in-pictures/… loads.
